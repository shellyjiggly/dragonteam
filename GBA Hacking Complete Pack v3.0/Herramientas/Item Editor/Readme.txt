 ===========================================================================================
					-DESCRIPTION-
 ===========================================================================================

			A program to edit the items in GBA Pokémon ROMs.
         Please note that this program is thethethethe's but he gave permission to work on it.

 ===========================================================================================
				       -ROMs SUPPORTED-
 ===========================================================================================
		
	         At the moment only English, French, Italian, and Spanish R/S/FR/LG/E 
                                         ROMs are supported.		

 ===========================================================================================
				       -INSTRUCTIONS-
 ===========================================================================================

 1) Open the ROM you want to edit.
 2) Click on the item that you want to edit.
 3) Then change what you want.
 4) Click "Save" and your done!
 
*Note*	If you click on another item in the listbox the data for the previous one will 
not be saved. Make sure to Click "Save" before you click on another item.
